AI Rules of Engagement

● Permitted

Logic & Debugging

Use AI for Excel formula troubleshooting and Red Teaming your investment logic.

Data Summaries

Cross-check AI-generated economic summaries against FRED or FactSet before use.

● Prohibited

Direct Generation

Copying AI portfolios or risk metrics without raw data verification is strictly prohibited.

⚠️ The Hallucination Clause: Every AI-generated number must be traceable to a human-verified source.
